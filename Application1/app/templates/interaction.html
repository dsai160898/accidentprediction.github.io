Interactive Dashboard

We have created this map that allows you to find accident hotspots along the driving route between two locations at a particular time.

To use the map we need to give the following inputs
(1) a date and time
(2) Starting location
(3) Destination

Your route will be shown on the map. We will then feed all these inputs to our predictive model, which will give us the corresponding road accident risk on the map.
