Prediction of Road Traffic Accidents

Road Traffic Accidents are a major cause of death worldwide leading to around 1.35 million deaths and 50 million injuries every year that is, one person is killed every 25 seconds.

Hence, we aim to serve you with this platform which wil give you an idea about all the accidents that may occur on your route of interest and you plan your journey accordingly.
